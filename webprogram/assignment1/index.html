Shomoy James

Shomoy James, an 18 year old with a passion for computers and writing, starts his journey here!

His favourite things

  1. Reading or Writing Stories:

    For a boy who grew up reading a lot, Shomoy has developed extraordinary reading skills at a young age. Fascinated with the stories he's read over the years, some stories he's come across didn't have the ending he hoped for. That made him wonder what it would be like to create the ending he wanted. And, for the first time in high school, Shomoy decided to rewrite a story he's already read before, and it felt much more satisfying and sensible to him. That was the moment he realized that his passion for reading could also be turned into a passion for writing, and that was the moment he started brainstorming idea for his own stories.

  2. Video Games:

    Life tends to get overwhelming at times, and playing video games is a great way for Shomoy to relax and unwind. It allows him to escape reality temporarily and immerse himself in a different world. Whether he's playing a Batman game or something that requires more strategy, it helps him to clear his mind, or to simply have fun and enjoy himself.

  3. Watching Batman:

    Batman is Shomoy's true role model, and full inspiration. He grew up watching Batman movies, playing Batman games, and reading Batman comics. Shomoy admires Batman's intelligence, determination, and true commitment to justice. Batman's ability to rise above his own personal struggles and make a positive impact on the world is something that Shomoy finds truly inspiring. He hopes to inquire the same qualities of courage, resilience, and compassion that Batman represents in his own life.
